Partner Terms / Network Onboarding Agreement (v1.0)
Last updated: 2026-03-25

1. Scope
These Partner Terms govern the commercial relationship between Print Price Pro SIA (“PrintPrice Pro”, “we”, “us”) and the printing company or production partner (“Partner”, “you”).

2. Nature of the Relationship
PrintPrice Pro operates a production intelligence system. The Partner remains the independent production entity.

3. Partner Admission
Admission is conditional on qualification and criteria verification by PrintPrice Pro.

4. Partner Obligations
Maintain accurate information, produce jobs within specifications, and use only approved system workflows.

5. PrintPrice Pro Obligations
Orchestrate routing and provide valid production assets to the Partner.

6. No Volume Guarantee
Admission to the network does not guarantee any minimum jobs or exclusivity.

7. Job Acceptance
Routed jobs become binding only when accepted in the system.

8. Quality and Non-Conformity
Partner is responsible for production quality and remediation costs if standards are not met.

9. Pricing and Fees
Commercial terms are defined in the specific onboarding schedules.

10. Non-Circumvention
Partners must not bypass the platform for introduced customers.

11. Confidentiality
All non-public data must be treated as confidential.

12. Intellectual Property
Files and metadata remain owned by the respective customers.

13. Data Protection
Parties must comply with GDPR and relevant regulations.

14. System Use
Credentials must be protected and system integrity monitored.

15. Audit
PrintPrice Pro may monitor logs for security and quality purposes.

16. Termination
Either party may terminate the relationship with notice or immediately for breach.

17. Governing Law
Latvian law applies with exclusive jurisdiction in Riga.
